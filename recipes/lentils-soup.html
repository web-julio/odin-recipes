Lentils Soup

Hearty lentil soup, chock full of veggies and very yummy. Serve with warm cornbread.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of dry lentils are the main event
  • 8 cups of water
  • Garden-fresh veggies like celery (2 stalks), carrots (2 diced), and onion (1 chopped)
  • Bay leaf, dried oregano, and basil add aroma and depth ( 1 tablesppon each)
  • crushed tomatoes (1 can) and vinegar (2 tablespoons)
  • salt and pepper to taste

How do you make it ?

  1. Heat oil in a large soup pot over medium heat.
  2. Add onions, carrots, and celery; cook and stir until onion is tender, 3 to 5 minutes. overhead of sauteed mirepoix in pot
  3. Stir in garlic, bay leaf, oregano, and basil; cook for 2 minutes
  4. Stir in lentils, and add water and tomatoes. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and let simmer until lentils are tender, at least 1 hour
  5. Stir in vinegar and season with salt and pepper; taste and adjust as needed.
